About Asad Milk Shop

Three Decades of Purity

Asad Milk Shop was founded in 1990 in Faisalabad, Pakistan, with a simple mission: to bring pure, fresh and authentic dairy products to every household in the city. For over three decades, we have proudly served generations of families with our commitment to quality and tradition.

We specialize in traditional dairy delicacies made using time-honored recipes passed down through the years, including fresh milk, yogurt, Rabri Doodh, Doodh ki Bottles, Gagrala, Son Halwa and Khir — each prepared with care using the finest ingredients sourced locally from trusted farms.

Beyond everyday home delivery, Asad Milk Shop is a trusted name for catering large scale events. We proudly provide our dairy products and traditional sweets for marriage ceremonies, walima functions, birthday parties, corporate events, religious gatherings and other special occasions — delivering fresh, high-quality products in bulk quantities right on time.
